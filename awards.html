Awards

I have received many awards over the course of my high school career.

The President's Award for Educational Excellence

This award was given to me in both my Freshman, Sophomore, and Junior years. It is awarded to those who are to earn a grade point average of 90 on a 100 point scale and either a teacher recommendation or a high achievement in reading or math on state tests or nationally-normed tests.

The Archbishop Riordan Freshman Marianist Award

This award is given to those who have completed the most service hours in my Freshman class. I received this award because I have fulfilled a total of 230 hours in my Freshman year.

Certificates of Academic Achievement

I received numerous Certificates of Academic Excellence over the course of my high school career in various subjects. Teachers nominate those who have excelled in their respective courses. I have received certificates for DV Art, Fabrication Lab, and Art II.
